## Data Availability

The dataset used in this study consists of publicly available YouTube video metadata collected from the official channels of Al Jazeera, BBC News, Deutsche Welle, and TRT World. It includes 2,370 YouTube Shorts published between 7 October 2023 and 7 October 2024. The dataset contains video-level metadata only (e.g., video identifiers, titles, publication timestamps, view counts, and descriptions) and does not include the video files themselves.

Download the dataset from Zenodo (available after publication).
